About MSS+A

M.S. SATSANGI & ASSOCIATES is headed by Architect Prof. M.S. Satsangi, who has put together a group of highly experienced, and young enthusiastic professionals to pursue projects and provide services in related disciplines of:

  • Architecture
  • Structural Design
  • Landscape Design
  • Town Planning
  • Interior Design

Since the year 2000, this architectural practice has been designing and building mainly Educational, Institutional Campuses including Hospitals along with other official, residential, industrial, commercial and religious projects.

We, as a group, have further expanded our working capacity by associating with a panel of Associate Architects, Specialist Associates as well as advisors/ consultants in various disciplines/ fields for specific tasks so as to deliver comprehensive services as and when it is called for.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

  • Environmentally responsive architecture, incorporating the best of contemporary techniques and technologies to create design identities to address ever-changing conditions from time to time through timeless solutions.
  • Conceiving building designs not only from architecture view point but with the interiors in mind to give a complete picture in terms of its functioning and credibility.
  • Comprehensively derived minimalistic approach to deliver appropriate, efficient and relevant design solutions.
  • Inherent scope for in-built flexibility to provide various multi- purpose solutions within same building complex for re-adaptive uses.
  • To reduce foot print in order to minimize ground coverage and in retaining the obvious advantage of green cover.
  • Aimed at Nature blend visually pleasing & appealing designs to create a perfect aesthetic balance between man, nature and built environment in line with context, resulting in form of Visual Pride.
  • Emphasis on maintenance- free environment/ minimal maintenance design strategies with economy logistics in place, clubbed with health conscious solutions to keep desired human aspirations, providing conditions of comfort and high spirits that ensures Client satisfaction.
